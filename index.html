Our Story

Power.
Precision.Revolution.

Founded in 2005, Drum Revolution is an electrifying taiko ensemble based in the San Francisco Bay Area, known for blending traditional Japanese taiko drumming with modern, innovative rhythms and choreography. Rooted in cultural tradition yet forward-thinking in its approach, Drum Revolution brings a fresh, diverse spirit to the local arts scene, creating a pulse that resonates with the Bay Area's rich multicultural landscape. Whether in festivals, community events, or intimate concerts, they ignite the stage with passion and unity, embodying the heart of a true musical revolution.

Drum Revolution has grown into one of the largest taiko groups in the San Francisco Bay Area, offering classes and workshops in six locations: Emeryville, Millbrae, Palo Alto, Los Altos, Milpitas, and Los Gatos. The group performs all over the Bay Area, with major appearances at the San Francisco Cherry Blossom Festival, the Cupertino Cherry Blossom Festival, the Millbrae Japanese Culture Festival, for the Oakland Earthquakes, at the Rugby World Cup Sevens, and for various festivals, firms, schools, and cities across the Bay Area.

Training

Find Your
Rhythm

Adult
ADULT 1A CLASSES
This course engages in uchikomi based practice. The one who has either a sense of rhythm or a stamina to hit taiko for two hours are welcome. In each class, everyone will participate by playing on actual drums. The class meets once a week.
Adult
ADULT 1B CLASSES
These classes are designed for individuals with advanced knowledge and understanding of taiko theory. It will improve the player's power, speed, rhythm, and other important aspects of drumming skills. These courses require individual discipline to formulate group uniformity, which leads to sound unity, as well as physical strength.
